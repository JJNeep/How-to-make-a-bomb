How to Make a Bomb

Steps:

  1. Locate any highly combustible or reactive substances in the household.
  2. Create detonators and fuses to help with the KABOOM part of the bomb.
  3. If the bomb you are making is nuclear, you will need to set up specialized detonators to drive a nuclear substance of your choice into a larger clump of the same nuclear substance. This heavy collision will trigger a nuclear meltdown and explode.
  4. Make sure that your detonators are fully set up and have a fuse ready to connect to a Remote Detonation Device (RDD).
  5. Get to a safe distance, if the bomb is nuclear (100 - 500km), and when ready click the button and enjoy a bomb!

Remember, this information is provided for educational purposes only. Creating explosives is illegal and poses significant risks to safety.
